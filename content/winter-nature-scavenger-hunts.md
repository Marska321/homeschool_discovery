# 10 Nature Scavenger Hunts for Winter

Winter is a wonderful time to explore nature! Even when the leaves have fallen and the air is crisp, there is so much to discover outdoors.

## Why Winter?

Many people think winter is a dead season, but that's far from the truth. In fact:
- Animal tracks are easier to spot in snow or mud.
- The structure of trees becomes visible without their leaves.
- You can observe different types of birds that stick around for the colder months.
